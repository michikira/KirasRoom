What's Plus-Tech Squeeze Box?

PSB is a Japanese alternative rock/edm duo that started in around 1997. Their music is hard to describe with just one genre, but most people will describe it as shibuya kei or picopop. Hayashibe Tomonori, one of the members, explained that he describes his music as "panic pop".
